KA Language Progress Report

The KA Language Progess report shows progress the Internationalization initiavies have made to full-fill the Milestones (Test,Live,Rockstar) they are tracking. Criterias are defined in Translations milestones (lite, test / alpha, live / beta).

Crowdin

Crowdin Progress lists all translation files (.po) required for the specified target. All required with 100% translation status are hidden.

Missing Videos

Lists all KA Subjects where dubbing of Videos is required.

Missing Subtitles

Lists all KA Subject where subtitles for videos are required and dubbing is not mandatory but optional.
